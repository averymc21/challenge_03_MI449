Project HelioSphere

  • Status: Active
  • Location: Solar System - Orbiting Mars
  • The experiment aims to understand how the solar wind affects Mars' atmospheric composition and its potential implications for future human missions to the planet.

Galactic Voyager

  • Status: Active
  • Location: Interstellar Space
  • The spacecraft has successfully passed the heliopause and is currently transmitting data back to Earth for analysis. Preliminary findings suggest a complex interaction between the solar wind and the interstellar medium.

Dark Matter Mapping Initiative

  • Status: Inactive - Data Analysis Phase
  • Location: Virgo Cluster
  • The experiment utilized gravitational lensing techniques and spectroscopic observations to trace the invisible dark matter structure. Preliminary data suggests a complex and intricate web of dark matter filaments connecting galaxies within the cluster.
